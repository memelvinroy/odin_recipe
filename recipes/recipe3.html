INGREDIANTS

  • 1 cup honey
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger

STEPS

  1. In a bowl,mix together honey,ketchup,mustard,Worcestershire sauce, minced onion, cloves, and ginger
  2. Pour over ham slices as a glaze.
